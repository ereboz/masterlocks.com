How do I set or reset the combination on my backpack lock model #1550?

Here are the instructions on how to reset the combination on your backpack lock (which is pre-set at the factory to open to combination 0-0-0):
  1. Align dials to preset combination (0-0-0)
  2. While pushing in button located on the bottom of the lock, turn the dials to any combination you choose
  3. Release button ONLY after you have finished choosing your combination
  4. The lock is now ready to be used. To change your new combination again, dial your combination and repeat steps 2-3

How do I reset a "Set-Your-Own" Word combination cable?

Here are instructions for resetting your "Set-Your-Own" Word combination cable:
  1. Spin the wheels until you decide on a 4 or 5-letter word for your new combination
  2. Dial the combination wheels to read R-I-D-E-R and withdraw the cable
  3. Turn the knob 90 degrees in a clockwise direction until it stops
  4. Reset the combination wheels to the desired word making sure the letters line up with the marks on the case
  5. Once you have selected your word combination, turn the knob back to the original position in a counterclockwise direction
  6. Your new combination is now set

How do I reset a "Set-Your-Own" combination cable?

Here are instructions for resetting your "Set-Your-Own" combination cable
  1. Open the lock using the factory set combination
  2. Flip the reset lever to the up position to reset your combination
  3. Reset the combination making sure the desired numbers are lined up with the pointer
  4. Flip the reset lever to the down position to complete the setting of the combination, making sure you do not move the number dials
  5. Test the new combination by fully inserting the lock prong into the cylinder hole and read the number

How do I set or reset the combination on my "Set-Your-Own" combination bike or cable lock?

  • Model #8114
    1. Open the lock and withdraw the cable
    2. Turn the collar (circular, ribbed and in front of the combination wheels) - clockwise until it stops (about halfway around)
    3. Set the combo wheels to your desired combination
    4. Turn the collar counterclockwise until it stops at its original position
    5. Record your new combination in a safe place
  • Model #8119, 8120, and 8122
    1. Open the lock and withdraw the cable
    2. Flip the small reset lever by the combination wheels to the "up" position
    3. Set the combo wheels to your desired combination, making sure the numbers are lined up with the marks on the case
    4. Flip the reset lever "down" to the starting position
    5. Record your new combination in a safe place.
  • Model #8280 (Pro-Sport)
    1. Press the button and remove u-bar from lock body
    2. Open the anti-dust cover and align the opening combination to the guide points
    3. Push the red button towards notch in body. Hold down (there should be a clicking sound)
    4. Turn the dials to your desired combination
    5. Pull back reset button to its original position. The lock should now be reset to your new combination
    6. Record your new combination in a safe place
