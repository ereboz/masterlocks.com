Is my product covered under any Master Lock warranty? How do I find that information?

There are many different warranties that may apply, depending on the product. Please follow these instructions to find your product warranty:

  1. Go to the keyword and product search in the upper right on masterlock.com
  2. Enter your product number
  3. Go to the product detail page
  4. Click on the “Product Warranty” link under the description
  5. If you don’t know your product number, just search on your product type (such as “Luggage Lock”) and use the results to find your product.
